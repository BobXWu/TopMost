Towards the TopMost: A Topic Modeling System Toolkit
===================================

TopMost provides complete lifecycles of topic modeling, including dataset preprocessing, model training, testing and evaluations. It covers the most popular topic modeling scenarios, like static, dynamic, hierarchical, and cross-lingual topic modeling.

| This is our demo paper of `TopMost: TopMost: A Topic Modeling System Toolkit <./>`_.
| This is our survey on neural topic models: `A Survey on Neural Topic Models: Methods, Applications, and Challenges <https://assets.researchsquare.com/files/rs-3049182/v1_covered_557a6d7a-c326-45c0-98e2-028fb25af7ba.pdf?c=1686720142>`_.
